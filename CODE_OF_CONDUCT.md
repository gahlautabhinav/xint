# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Be respectful, constructive, and welcoming. Focus on the work.

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or ban contributors who are
disruptive, abusive, or act in bad faith.

## Scope

Applies to all project spaces: GitHub issues, PRs, discussions, and any
communication representing this project.

## Reporting

Report problems to the maintainer at **abhi.gahlaut69@gmail.com**. Reports are
reviewed confidentially.
